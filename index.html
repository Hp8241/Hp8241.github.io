Research Experience

Personal Course Paper: "Analysis of the Impact and Mechanisms of Social Activity Participation on Elderly Mental Health—An Empirical Study Based on CHARLS 2020 Data"
September 2023 - January 2024
In the context of rapid aging in China, the elderly population is growing rapidly, and elderly health has become an urgent issue. This paper, based on CHARLS data from 2020, 2018, and 2011, obtained 7,581 valid samples after data cleaning and variable selection. Using instrumental variables to address endogeneity issues, it aims to explore the impact and channels of social activity participation on the mental health of the elderly.

Group Course Paper: "Exploring the Impact of 5A-level Tourist Attractions Selection on Air Quality—A Case Study of Tourist Attractions in Guangdong Province"
September 2023 - January 2024
With the full reopening post-pandemic, China's tourism industry has developed rapidly, and the economic and environmental benefits brought by the 5A scenic spot label effect have attracted much attention. This paper uses all 4A and 5A-level scenic spots in Guangdong Province from 2004 to 2021 as the research sample, with the annual average concentration of PM2.5 and PM10 as proxies for air quality. It employs a multiple-period difference-in-differences method to explore the impact of the transition from 4A to 5A-level on the air quality of the scenic spots.
- Responsible for the paper's theme conception, identification model construction, robustness testing, and conclusion section.
